About

Skills

Code with purpose.

After graduating from Eastern University with a BA in Political Science, I spent eight years as a community organizer for non-profit and grassroots organizations in Philadelphia. I began my self-taught coding journey in 2018 through free resources online. Since I love working with people and leveling up, I applied to the Penn LPS Coding Bootcamp.

I was born in Lima, Peru and identify as a queer migrant woman of Color. I'm also a salsa dancer. I'm passionate about collective growth, a lot of laughter, and creating exciting and transformative things together. If I'm not coding, dancing, or lifting heavy things at the gym, I can be found hanging out with my two cats, Serena and Sofia.
